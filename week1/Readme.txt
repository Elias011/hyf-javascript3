
This project is first project of week1 of Javascript3 
It is a SPA-project to show you an information about "Hack your future" repositories on Githup and also show you a summary about "Hack your future" curriculum 
The application is very simple there are two button the first called "HYF Repositories" when you click on it , it will show you the names of  "Hack your future" repositories  with a link for each repositories on Github. 
The second button is search button when you write a name of one of the repositories and you click on search you will get a short summary of this repository with a photo of the contributor . 
Note:this project is the same project in the last week of javascript2 but here I used the functional programming in javascript.
